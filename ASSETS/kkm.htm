POCO F6 Series and POCO Pad Launch: Full Specs, Pricing, and Essential Details Revealed

Kartikey Singh
May. 23, 2024, 19:40

Xiaomi’s sub-brand, Poco, has just announced its new flagship killer for 2024, the Poco F6 series, globally, and the Poco F6 in India. These smartphones pack some of the best hardware in a mid-premium device at a not-so-premium price.

POCO F6 Details

The Poco F6 features a 6.67-inch 12-bit 120Hz 1.5K OLED display. This display supports HDR 10+ and Dolby Vision, and boasts a peak HDR brightness of 2400 nits. To protect your eyes, the display also features 1920Hz PWM dimming and TÜV Rheinland certification. Additionally, it is protected by Gorilla Glass Victus and supports wet touch functionality. The smartphone is powered by the latest Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 chipset, which boasts an AnTuTu score of over 1.5 million points. With such high performance, cooling is essential; hence, the Poco F6 includes the brand’s first-ever Ice Loop cooling technology for efficient heat management.

For photography enthusiasts, the smartphone offers a dual-camera setup featuring a 50MP IMX 882 OIS and an 8MP IMX 355 sensor. It supports 4K 60FPS video recording. Thanks to the AI capabilities of the Snapdragon 8s Gen 3, the device introduces several new AI features such as AI Expansion, AI Magic Eraser Pro, AI Bokeh, AI air gestures, and more. The battery life also sounds promising, with a 5000mAh battery plus 90W charging, allowing it to easily last a day. Additionally, it includes dual stereo speakers that support Dolby Atmos, enhancing the multimedia experience alongside the vibrant display. The Poco F6 also features an IP64 dust and water resistance rating.

The smartphone was launched in three different variants in India and in two variants globally. All variants are equipped with LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.0 storage.

Pricing

  • 8GB 256GB: ₹29,999 / $379
  • 12GB 256GB: ₹31,999
  • 12GB 512GB: ₹33,999 / $429

POCO F6 Pro Details

The Poco F6 Pro features a flagship-level 6.67-inch 120Hz 12-bit 2K display. Just like its sibling, the F6, this display also supports HDR10+ and Dolby Vision playback and boasts a whopping 4000 nits of peak HDR brightness. The display also features advanced eye protection care with its 3840 PWM dimming technology and is TÜV Rheinland certified. The F6 Pro is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 flagship chipset, boasting an AnTuTu score of over 1.6 million points, and supports Liquid Cool Technology 4.0.

Photography has taken a significant leap with the F6 Pro over its predecessors. It features a 50MP 1/1.55′ Light Fusion 800 OIS sensor, along with an 8MP ultra-wide and a 2MP macro lens. The new sensor can capture stunning pictures, whether day or night, thanks to its advanced Ultra Night Algorithms. Like its younger sibling, the F6, the phone features a range of AI capabilities and AIGC support. The build quality and texture have also seen major improvements; the F6 Pro features a 3D glass curved design and a premium metal frame.

The smartphones feature a 5000mAh battery plus 120W charging combo to ensure you never run out of power. In conclusion, both the Poco F6 and F6 Pro are a gamer’s paradise thanks to their supreme performance. Poco also promises three years of Android OS updates and four years of security patches for both the Poco F6 and F6 Pro.

The Poco F6 Pro offers some of the best flagship specifications at a very affordable price. The phone is available in three variants globally, and all variants feature a combination of LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.0 storage.

Pricing

  • 12GB 256GB: $499
  • 12GB 512GB: $549
  • 16GB 1024GB: $629

POCO Pad Details

The Poco Pad marks the brand’s first entry into the already competitive global tablet market. The Poco Pad features a large 12.1-inch 120Hz 2.5K high-resolution display with 600 nits of brightness. The display also supports HDR 10 and Dolby Vision for everyday content consumption. It is powered by the Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 chipset, which is sufficient for performing everyday tasks without any lag or stutters. The tablet excels in multimedia experiences with its quad speakers that support Dolby Atmos. The Poco Pad also boasts substantial endurance power thanks to its 10000mAh battery and 33W charging support. One of the main highlights of this tablet is that it’s powered by Xiaomi’s HyperOS. HyperOS ensures seamless interconnectivity between smartphone and tablet experiences, thus elevating the productivity of the tablet.

The Poco Pad comes with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage, priced at $329 globally. Poco has also launched tablet accessories, including a keyboard for $79, a Poco Smartpen for $59, and a Pad Cover for $19.

The Poco Pad will also be launched in India in the upcoming months, so stay tuned.
